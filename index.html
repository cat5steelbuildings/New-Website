Common Questions

Quick answers.

Do I pay full price up front?
No. On most residential builds, all we ask for is a deposit. You inspect the finished build, and the balance is paid only after you're satisfied with what we delivered. Larger commercial scopes may have different terms — your rep will walk you through what fits your project.
How fast can you install?
Most buildings ship in 4–6 weeks from order. Install typically takes 1–3 days once on site. We handle scheduling, delivery, and crew so you don't have to coordinate vendors.
Do you handle permits?
Yes. Permit handling is included in every turnkey package across FL, GA, and AL. We pull the permits, file site plans, and coordinate with your county so you don't have to.
What financing options do you offer?
We work with three financing types so we can match every buyer to the right option.

Tier 1 — Florida residents: Launch Credit Union, up to 120% financing for up to 120 months at sharper-than-bank rates.

Tier 2 — FL/GA/AL residents with credit: Home improvement loans through HFS Financial and Acorn Finance — marketplace-style with multiple lenders competing. Up to 120% of project cost, terms up to 20 years, 0% APR promotional offers available, soft credit check only.

Tier 3 — No credit check needed: Rent-to-own programs, available on most plans. Small upfront payment, flexible monthly terms, every payment builds toward ownership. Cancel anytime.

Your rep will walk you through which option fits best on a no-pressure consult.
What's the wind rating on a Cat 5 build?
We build in 170 MPH wind certification — engineered to handle Category 4 to borderline Category 5 winds. Optional 180 MPH certification is also available for coastal locations and high-wind zones. Your rep will recommend the right rating for your specific site and county code.
What's the difference between 14-gauge and 12-gauge framing?
12-gauge is thicker, stronger, and comes with a 20-year structural warranty. We recommend 12-gauge for any building exposed to coastal winds, large openings, or commercial use. 14-gauge is the standard.
Do you really price match?
Yes — on comparable turnkey quotes (same size, same gauge, same wind rating, same included services). Bring us the quote and we'll match or beat it. Caveat: we won't match a "building only" price against our turnkey, because that's apples to oranges.
What's "Guaranteed Lower Than" mean?
It's exactly what it sounds like — every plan is guaranteed to come in below the listed price for that build configuration. Final price varies by your location, options, and quote date, but it'll never exceed the price shown. That's our promise.
